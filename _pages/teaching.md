Served as a co-instructor for several undergraduate courses at the Department of Computer Science & Engineering, CUET. Conducted both theory lectures and laboratory sessions, and was responsible for designing and grading weekly assignments, class tests, and final examinations. Mentored and guided undergraduate students throughout the courses.
